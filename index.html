Dear [Greeting name]

The financial services industry faces the challenges and opportunities of meeting rising expectations from a new generation of consumers. We all know that the march of the digital consumer experience is relentless and that financial institutions face the impacts of global connectivity and 21st century consumerism.

The pressures faced by financial institutions in this new normal demand an exceptional level of understanding of what customers really expect of their primary banking provider. To this end, FIS recently launched a global study to gain insight by measuring bank performance to customer expectations for 18 key measures.

The newly launched FIS Consumer Banking PACE Index™ enables banking providers to understand where consumer perceptions of banks fall short and how to address the shortfalls with the greatest positive effect. We found that more than 75% of banked consumers globally cite a gap across a range of factors essential to customer trust and brand perception.
